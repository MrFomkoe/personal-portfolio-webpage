About me

Hello! My name is Roman Ponomarjov. I'm Latvia based front-end developer taking on the path of programming.

At the age of 29, I decided to make crucial changes in my life and change my occupation. After taking JavaScript courses at Mācības pieaugušajiem ("Studies for adults" EU project) in September 2021 I made a conclusion that programming actually interests me and I'd wish to link my life with it.

The next step in the study of programming was courses at Codecademy within the Front-End Engineer path. My objective to complete is to achieve sufficient knowledge and skills to start working as a junior developer.

In my free time, I play compose and play music as well as read and play video games.
